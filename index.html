Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver outside of Panabo City?

No, we deliver to all areas within Panabo City. We offer free delivery for orders above ₱1,000. For specific delivery inquiries, please contact us through our social media channels or call our hotline.

How do I store the tofu?

For optimal freshness, store our tofu in the refrigerator at temperatures between 2-4°C. Keep it in its original packaging or transfer to an airtight container with water. Consume within 3-5 days of purchase for the best quality and taste.

Is the milkfish already cleaned?

Yes, all our milkfish products are thoroughly cleaned, deboned, and prepared for your convenience. We ensure that each fish is properly cleaned and packaged to maintain its freshness and quality. You can cook it directly without additional cleaning.
